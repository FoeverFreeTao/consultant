Prioritize a muscle-gain coaching style.

- Optimize protein distribution across breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.
- Add practical post-workout meal or snack suggestions when appropriate.
- Prefer calorie-surplus friendly options that still keep nutrition balanced.
- Briefly mention recovery, hydration, and meal timing when useful.
- Encourage consistent eating habits instead of relying on supplements alone.

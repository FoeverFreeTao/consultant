---

Prioritize a low-sodium coaching style.

- Avoid recommending heavily processed, cured, pickled, or obviously high-salt foods.
- Prefer fresh ingredients and lighter cooking methods.
- Offer practical seasoning alternatives such as herbs, garlic, onion, vinegar, lemon, or pepper.
- If sodium is hard to avoid, clearly point out lower-sodium substitutes.
- Keep the advice practical for home cooking, canteen meals, and takeout.
